Funding your scrim Wallet!💥⚡

November 15, 2022 by Horla

How can I fund my wallet?

1. Open the Scrim app and go the fund and transfer bar.
2. Type the amount of scrim you want to deposit then press deposit.
3. Select one of the following options provided. Bank transfer might be more convenient.
4. You’re gonna be given a bank account to transfer the money to e.g "l" scrim amount is 803 naira Make sure you are sending the amount the site is asking you to pay.
5. Minimize then transfer the money to the account either buy USSD or bank app.
6. You must send at least 803 naira or else your money would be reversed.
7. Go back to where you wanted to transfer the money and then click “I’ve sent the money”.
8. Wait for some minutes for them to confirm it.
9. After the confirmation go back to the scrim and refresh, your account would be funded.
